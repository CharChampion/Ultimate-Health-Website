BPM Calculator


What is BPM?

BPM stands for Beats Per Minute. A person's BPM is used to measure the number of heartbeats per minute (60 seconds).


Instructions To Calculate


  1. Check for your pulse, somewhere around the palm side of your wrist, or the front bottom of your neck.
  2. Set a timer for 10 seconds, and count how many heartbeats happened in those 10 seconds.
  3. Enter the number of heartbeats that happened in those 10 seconds into the textbox below.
