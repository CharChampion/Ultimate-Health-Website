Leg Exercise Picker


Here, the Leg Exercise Picker will pick two random exercises, and tell you how many of the 2 exercises you need to do. The Leg Exercise Picker will allow you to maintain your exercise schedule without getting bored of doing the same exercise everyday. Below is the list of all the 6 leg exercises.


  1. Squats
  2. Lunges
  3. Calf Raises
  4. Stair Climbing
  5. Split Lunges
  6. Donkey Kicks

Instructions

You will need to roll 2 dice[s] first. Next, you will need to enter the number of the first dice and the second dice into the 2 given textboxes.
